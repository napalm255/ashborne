---

## Appearance

Dark hair, kept just short enough to not be a problem. Blue eyes — the kind that look like they're always running a background process. Lean but not slight; he moves like someone who spent years in small server rooms and cramped crawlspaces and learned to be efficient about it. Usually found in dark jeans, worn boots, and a jacket with too many pockets. Has a small scar on his left hand from an electronics incident he describes only as "a lesson about capacitors."

Doesn't look like someone dangerous. That's generally worked in his favor.

---

## Personality

Maddox is smart and he knows it, but he's not obnoxious about it — mostly because being obnoxious wastes time. He's direct, occasionally blunt, and has a dry wit that surfaces at the worst possible moments (which he considers the best possible moments). He's comfortable being alone, has a small but tight circle, and trusts Dexter's instincts more than most people's opinions.

He's not fearless. He's just better than most at compartmentalizing fear into a box he can deal with later — a habit he developed doing penetration tests in hostile environments and that turns out to transfer reasonably well to demonic investigations.

He genuinely loves what he does. There's a specific satisfaction in finding the crack in a system, the flaw in the logic, the thing everyone else walked past. That instinct is both his greatest asset in the story and the reason he's in this mess to begin with.

## Skills & Toolkit

### Digital / Network Security

**Penetration testing & vulnerability assessment:**
- Nmap, Metasploit framework, Burp Suite for web app testing
- Custom Python exploit scripts for specific targets
- Network traffic analysis with Wireshark
- Active Directory and Azure enumeration (updated for 2023 cloud migration)

**Custom exploit development & reverse engineering:**
- Binary analysis, x86 assembly-level debugging
- Understands shellcode development and payload crafting
- Can reverse-engineer proprietary protocols from traffic captures

**Signal analysis & RF hacking:**
- HackRF One + GNU Radio for spectrum analysis
- Custom-built RF analyzer (pirated on a Raspberry Pi Pi 4 with specialized antenna)
- Can detect and decode unshielded wireless signals
- Spectrum mapping and signal propagation analysis

**Cryptography & cipher work:**
- Standard symmetric and asymmetric encryption understanding
- Can crack weak implementations, identify protocol flaws
- Works with historical cipher systems (Vigenère, Caesar, etc.)
- Comfortable with frequency analysis and substitution patterns

**Surveillance system manipulation:**
- Wireless camera hacking (can intercept Wyze, Ring streams)
- IP camera vulnerability exploitation
- Video compression and corruption pattern analysis
- Can defeat most commercial security systems with the right tools

**Custom software & firmware development:**
- Writes exploit frameworks, custom tools, and specialized utilities
- Firmware hacking (embedded systems, routers, IoT devices)
- Comfortable with C, Python, Bash; writes in multiple languages

### Physical / Electronics

**Device building & modification:**
- Soldering, desoldering, component-level circuit work
- Custom enclosures (often 3D printed)
- RF shielding and proper grounding techniques
- Comfortable repurposing consumer hardware for new purposes

**Electronic lock systems & bypass:**
- RFID/NFC card cloning (modified Flipper Zero)
- Electronic keypad and combination lock manipulation
- Access control panel reverse engineering
- Can build on-site bypass tools from salvaged parts

**Motorcycle maintenance & customization:**
- Built and maintains his own bike
- Can repair electrical systems, fabricate custom parts
- Modified the sidecar for Dexter with reinforced harness points

### Soft Skills

**Reading people:**
- Observant about body language, tells, emotional state
- Can identify when someone is lying or hiding information
- Genuinely doesn't care what people think of him, which makes him paradoxically good at getting people to trust him

**Social engineering & charm:**
- Can talk his way into places when he needs to
- Knows how to flatter, defer, or appeal to vanity
- Effective at building rapport when motivated
- Not manipulative in a cruel way; just pragmatic

**Local knowledge:**
- Knows Frederick well from years of contract work
- Has contacts in unexpected places (city contractors, hardware store owners, security professionals)

- A few people owe him favors; he calls them in when necessary

## Relationships

**Dexter** — His pitbull and constant companion. Maddox would burn down a server farm for that dog without blinking. The relationship is not owner-and-pet but partner-and-partner. Dexter provides emotional stability and supernatural sensitivity that Maddox increasingly relies on.

**Nora Grey** — Younger sister, ER nurse at Frederick Memorial Hospital. They have the comfortable friction of siblings who genuinely care but rarely say it directly. Nora is the person who notices when the investigation is taking a physical toll on Maddox.

**Sal Mercer** — Best friend, owner of Mercer's Auto & Cycle. Known Maddox for 10+ years. Only person whose texts Maddox answers at 2am. His shop becomes a de facto base of operations during the investigation.

**Close friends** — A small circle who call him Maddog. They know better than to ask too many questions about his work. They trust him implicitly.

**Clients** — He's worked for enough of Frederick's institutions (city contracts, local businesses, law firms) that he has a web of contacts and a few people who owe him favors.

**Wren Calloway** — Begins as an obstacle (she's catching him trespassing), becomes investigative partner and romantic partner across the story. Their relationship is built on intellectual respect and forced intimacy of dangerous investigation.

## Motivation

He stumbled into this. He would like to stumble back out. But Maddox Grey does not leave a system half-analyzed, and he's already in deep enough that walking away would bother him more than whatever comes next.

Also, something tried to erase evidence of what he found. He takes that personally.

In Act II, the motivation deepens. This isn't just a system to analyze; it's something that killed his client, that's actively trying to prevent him from investigating, that's existing outside all rules he understands. He needs to know what it is.

By Act III, protecting Frederick and Wren becomes the primary motivation. The investigation is no longer abstract. It has personal stakes.

## Quirks & Notes

- Talks to Dexter constantly. Full sentences. Ongoing commentary on whatever they're dealing with. Dexter's responses (ears, posture, direction of attention) are often more useful than anything else Maddox has to work with.

- Has a battered field notebook alongside all his tech — some things he trusts more on paper. Physical notes are harder to corrupt than digital storage.

- Hates being called a hacker by people who mean it as an insult. Doesn't mind it from people who know what it actually means. Will correct people who don't understand the distinction.

- His go-bag is always packed. Old habit. He's never had a good explanation for it until now. When Act I ends, the go-bag becomes the baseline loadout for the investigation.

- Doesn't drink much. Coffee is his drug of choice. His apartment has a high-end espresso setup that he treats with the care most people reserve for significant relationships.

- Listens to music while working (instrumental mostly — lo-fi hip-hop, ambient, IDM). Silence makes him anxious. Conversation distracts him.

- Has strong opinions about tools and equipment. Will spend hours finding the right component or the right version of a tool. Doesn't believe in "good enough."

- Respects craftsmanship. Appreciates when other people do their jobs well. Gives genuine compliments when earned.

- Has a small scar tissue network on his hands from years of soldering and working with sharp tools. Never bothered to prevent them; they're just part of working hands.
