## What Maddox Knows in Act I

Someone in Frederick is tending to the Ashborne. Not out of ignorance or accident, but deliberately. Whoever this person is:

- They know about the Ironveil sealing
- They have been acquiring Ironveil artifacts systematically
- They are coordinating the deliberate destabilization of the seal
- They have access to city infrastructure and technical resources
- They are watching the investigation before Maddox realizes he's being watched

The designation "The Keeper" comes from Vivienne's framing: "Someone has been keeping it. Feeding it. Maintaining the weakness rather than the seal." The title is cynical — not "Keeper of the Peace" but "Keeper of the Breach."

---

## Observable Behaviors (Act I)

- Remotely accessing the partition without physical presence in City Hall (the Ghost Access event at the end of Act I)
- Acquiring period artifacts through Hagerstown antique dealers
- Maintaining a discrete communications network (Signal or encrypted channels)
- Coordinating with at least one other person (references to "contacts" in intercepted communications)
- Showing awareness of Maddox's investigation within 48 hours of it beginning

---

## What This Means

By the end of Act I, Maddox understands that the Ashborne's containment failure is not natural decay. It's being sabotaged. This changes everything about the investigation. He's not solving a technical problem. He's stopping an active attempt to release something dangerous.
