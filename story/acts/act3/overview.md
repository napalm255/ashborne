# Act 3 — The Fracture

**Status**: Pre-production. Acts 1 and 2 documentation take priority.

**Act 3 Title**: The Fracture

**Timeframe**: June 2025 — final days (intensive, climactic)

**Premise**: The Ashborne begins directly influencing people. Maddox realizes it has been watching him since the partition. The final confrontation requires everything: all of his tools, all of the Ironveil's knowledge, and Wren's structural expertise to navigate sealed tunnels and execute the sealing ritual.

---

## Key Beats to Develop

- Frederick begins visibly changing — phenomenon intensity at maximum
- The Ashborne intrudes on Maddox's apartment network directly
- Sal and Nora become casualties of proximity (physical and psychological toll)
- Assembly of Tier 3 gear — Null Field Generator, Resonant Disruptor, Sealing Transmitter
- The cipher's final component discovery: Harmon House attic
- Assembling the complete sealing cipher from all collected fragments
- Construction of the Sealing Transmitter
- The warehouse confrontation — the Keeper's final stand
- The underground navigation — Wren leads through Frederick's tunnel network
- The sealing ritual execution at three sealing points (Best Farm, Libertytown, Middletown)
- Final confrontation with the Ashborne at the strongest manifestation point
- Resolution and aftermath — what Frederick looks like after

---

## Thematic Beats

- Isolation vs. partnership — Maddox must trust Wren completely
- Technology as tool and vulnerability — everything he uses can turn against him
- The seal cannot be broken once completed — this is containment, not destruction
- Personal cost — what does Maddox sacrifice to save Frederick?
