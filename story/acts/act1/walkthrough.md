# Act 1 Walkthrough Guide
## Player-Facing Navigation & Solutions

---

## Opening

You start in Maddox's workshop on the morning of April 8. Spend a few minutes exploring your home base:

- **Workstation**: Interact to manage inventory, crafting, and case files
- **Locked cabinet**: You'll need the code later; check back
- **Go-bag on bedroom floor**: Contains starting inventory (field notebook, slim jim set, fiber optic scope)
- **Dexter's bed**: Send Dexter on a Search command to discover the false kitchen cabinet (emergency cache with burner phone, cash)

## Story Progression Guide

### Days 1–2: The City Job
1. **Accept the contract** via Signal message
2. **Travel to City Hall** (use map on your phone to navigate; no fast travel yet)
3. **Enter the server room** with your contractor badge
4. **Run network scan** via your laptop (use Network Intrusion Kit to probe network)
5. **Find Rack Row C, Unit 7** — the anomalous partition will flag as unusual
6. **RF Analyzer task**: You need to build one. Return to workshop.

**Building the RF Analyzer (T2-01)**:
- Go to workbench
- Recipe shows: SDR module (in parts cabinet), wideband antenna (order online), microcontroller (order online), resin enclosure (print job)
- Build the custom IC first (requires components from parts cabinet)
- Queue print job for enclosure (ready on next workshop visit)
- Return next morning to complete assembly

**[TIP]** Check parts cabinet every visit — randomized components reset.

### Days 3–4: Hale's Dismissal & First Investigation
7. **Report findings to Hale** at City Hall — he dismisses the partition as junk
8. **Install surveillance tap** (T2-04) on network junction in server room anteroom
   - Requires Access Panel bypass puzzle (timed sequence with Network Intrusion Kit)
   - Guard shift change at 4 PM (12-minute window) is safest
9. **Take Carroll Creek route** via fast-travel (signals detection location)
10. **Use RF Analyzer** as you walk — anomaly will spike near bridge maintenance node

### Day 5: Death & Turning Point
11. **Receive news of Hale's death** via Nora's text
12. **Pull Verkada security feed** before contractor access expires (midnight)
13. **Download corrupted footage** — document metadata corruption pattern
14. **Return to apartment** and make the commitment to investigate

### Days 6–7: First Breakthrough
15. **Enter Colt & Compass Antiquities** (Patrick Street) — Dexter will be insistent about going here
16. **Show Vivienne the cipher pattern** (print it on paper; she'll recognize it immediately)
17. **Access archive** — she'll show you Civil War documents with matching cipher
18. **First mention of Ironveil** and the Ashborne

**[OPTIONAL]** Examine defibrillator cabinet in City Hall server room anteroom — Dexter will flag a symbol scratched into the drywall behind it. Photograph it; you can't decode it yet.

### Day 8: The Battlefield
19. **Travel to Monocacy Battlefield** after dark (trespassing is acceptable)
20. **Encounter Wren Calloway** on the path — she'll stop you
21. **Both dogs alert on same location** — Scout and Dexter's independent reaction is proof
22. **Wren lets you go** with a warning

### Day 9: Police Investigation
23. **Detective Okafor visits** your apartment — he's investigating Hale's death
24. **Provide limited information** — you can't give him everything without sounding insane
25. **Understand police pressure** is now a factor in your investigation

### Days 10–11: Historical Research
26. **Visit Historical Society** (West Patrick Street)
27. **Talk to Margaret Howell** (archivist) — build rapport by expressing genuine historical interest
28. **Access restricted archive** (Margaret will grant it based on rapport)
29. **Find the soldier photograph** (July 11, 1864, three with suspicious symbols on lapels)
30. **Zoom in on symbols** using your phone camera
31. **Cross-reference city directories** 1863–1866 to find "Harmon" family name

### Day 12: Connection
32. **Text Wren** about the photograph
33. **Meet at The Monocacy Pub** (evening) — dogs are outside
34. **Share data** — RF measurements, field observations, historical research align perfectly
35. **Understand this is real** and escalating

### Day 13: Final Evidence
36. **Return to City Hall after hours** (contractor badge still valid)
37. **Crack Hale's workstation password** (hint: family name + birth year in shorthand)
38. **Open TrueCrypt container** labeled "weird_notes"
39. **Review Hale's 14-month anomaly documentation** — the partition has been active and spreading
40. **Witness the Ghost Access event** (morning of April 19)
    - Monitoring terminal flags active session on partition
    - Terminal 7 in server room is unoccupied
    - Watch camera feed for 11 minutes of keyboard input from empty room
    - Signal Wren immediately

### Final Scene: The Commitment
41. **Document everything** in field notebook
42. **Make peace with investigating** something beyond your control
43. **Prepare for what comes next**

## Puzzle Solutions

**City Hall Access Panel Bypass**:
- Locate the panel in server room anteroom (Room 2B)
- Use Network Intrusion Kit to initiate bypass sequence
- Timed puzzle: ~30 seconds to complete (alarm triggers if you're too slow)
- Best approach: guard shift change at 4 PM (12-minute safe window)

**Hale's Workstation Password**:
- Hint: Look for personal details in his desk area
- Solution: Family name abbreviation + birth year (e.g., "Hale83" or similar)
- Cracks on second or third attempt typically

**TrueCrypt Container**:
- No password needed — encrypted volume mounts automatically once you locate it on Hale's encrypted drive
- Requires decryption key (located in Hale's workstation files as an encoded hint)

**Vivienne's Archive Access**:
- No puzzle — rapport-gated
- Talking about Frederick history and treating materials with care builds rapport
- After 1–2 visits, she opens deeper archive

## Collectibles & Documents

**Cipher Fragments** (found in Act 1):
- Three partial cipher components embedded in documents
- Photographing and documenting them adds to case files
- Act III requires assembling complete cipher

**Key Documents**:
- Confederate field document (Vivienne's archive)
- Soldier photograph with symbols (Historical Society)
- Hale's anomaly logs (his workstation)
- Your own field notebook observations

**Equipment to Build**:
- RF Signal Analyzer (T2-01) — essential
- Faraday Pouches (T1-04) — useful for protecting sensitive components
- Fiber Optic Scope (T1-03) — already have, useful for reconnaissance

## Act 1 → Act 2 Transition

By Act 1's end, you should have:
- RF Signal Analyzer built and tested
- Understanding of Ironveil and the Ashborne
- Working alliance with Wren
- Commitment to continuing investigation despite police pressure
- Identified Harmon family as Ironveil connection
- Ready to investigate Monocacy Battlefield interior (Best Farm)

Fast travel unlocks Monocacy Battlefield for Act II progression.

## Tips & Mechanics

**[TIP]** Dexter is your most reliable tool. When he goes still, pay attention. When he refuses to enter a zone, something is genuinely dangerous.

**[TIP]** RF Analyzer is your investigation core. Keep it active as you explore. Anomalies add to case files automatically.

**[TIP]** Save frequently by resting at the apartment. Story progression advances with each rest.

**[TIP]** Talk to Dexter constantly. The dialogue is genuine and occasionally provides hints about what to investigate next.

**[OPTIONAL]** Screenshot cipher patterns and symbols as you find them. Documenting visually helps with later pattern recognition.
